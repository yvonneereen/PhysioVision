01 Care that moves with you

From uncertainty
to confident movement.

PhysioVision turns the phone already in your home into an intelligent movement companion. No wearables. No complicated setup.

It watches joint alignment, counts repetitions, and gives one clear cue at a time—then shares meaningful trends with your physiotherapist.

Tell us what matters

The AI combines your goals, preferences, and personal movement calibration to provide guidance fitted to you.

The camera checks form in real time and responds with large visual cues and optional voice coaching.

Worsening trends prompt a therapist review, online consultation, and a safer adjusted plan.
